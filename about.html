ABOUT FUNK WORKSHOP

Funk Workshop was founded in late 2024 on the Dutch island of St. Maarten and made its debut in unforgettable fashion while grooving at a private party on the island of St. Barths. That night marked our entry into the exciting world of private events and mega yachts.

Known for delivering funky, feel-good covers of classic hits, our members bring over 90 years of combined musical experience to the stage. From tight rhythms to soulful solos, we know how to move a crowd.

Beyond live performances, Funk Workshop is deeply engaged in musical exploration. While we don’t produce in-house, we spend meaningful time in the studio refining our sound, experimenting with arrangements, and sharing knowledge through music instruction.

We’re an active performing group available for Private Parties, Weddings, Public Events, Indoor & Outdoor Functions, Hotel & Restaurant engagements, and more. Click Here to send us an email or contact us via whatsapp.
